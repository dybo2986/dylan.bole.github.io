Bio

Dylan Bole

I am a junior in the Environmental Design program at the University of Colorado Boulder, with an emphasis in product design. I am particularly drawn to lighting design and furniture design as tools for shaping atmosphere and function. Through my studies, I aim to create thoughtful, human-centered designs that balance aesthetic clarity with purposeful use.

Current Interests

Lighting design, furniture design, material warmth, and creating objects that bring comfort into everyday spaces.
